cinnamon buns

description

Ingredients

Bread Dough

  • 180 grams milk

    2 egg

    50 g sugar

    1/2 a stick butter, melted

    540 g all purpose flour

    1 tsp salt

    7 g instant yeast

Cinnamon Filling

  • half a stick softened butter

    1 cup dark brown sugar

    2 tbsps cinnamon

Directions

do first: take out butter to soften, melt butter, take out milk

  1. mix 180 grams milk, 2 egg , 50 grams sugar, half a stick melted butter

    separate mix 1 tsp salt, 7 grams instant yeast, 540 grams flour

    combine flour into wet ingredients

    knead dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth

    cover allow to rise on counter for 1 hour / doubled

    roll to rectangle 14x9, then spread half a stick softened butter on

    combine 1 cup dark brown sugar & 2 tbsps cinnamon

    sprinkle mixture over buttered dough and rub

    roll up dough tightly (ends will have less cinnamon)

    cut into 1 inch & place in a baking pan // note: use floss to cut for cleaner edges, or a serrated knife

    cover and let rise for 45 min

    bake at 350 for 25 minutes

    let cool for 5-10 minutes and enjoy!
