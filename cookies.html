chocolate chip cookies

description

Ingredients

Dry

  • 3 cups (360 g) flour

    1 tsps (5 g) baking soda

    2 tsp salt

Wet

  • 2 (1 brown:190g, 0.8 white:160g) cup sugar

    2 stick browned/melted butter

    2 egg

    2 tsp vanilla

    16 oz combined chocolate and toffee (if not toffee, use all chocolate)

Directions

do first: brown butter

  1. whisk 2 (1 brown:190g, 0.8 white:160g) cup sugar, 2 tsp salt, and 2 stick browned butter

  2. whisk in 2 egg and 2 tsp vanilla

  3. sift in 3 cups flour (360g) and 1 tsps (5g) baking soda, but not fully incorporated

  4. fold in 16 oz chocolate and toffee, or just chocolate

  5. rest for 36 hours in fridge (optiional, enhances flavor)

  6. bake at 350 for 12-15 min or 7 at 425 for a more goey cookie

  7. note/tips: use dark chocolate, resting makes it taste so much better, and if you want to keep the batter scoop first then freeze. no need to defrost
