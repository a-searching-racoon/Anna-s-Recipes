lemon cheesecake

description

Ingredients

Crust

  • 3/4 cups graham cracker crumbs

    1/4 cup almond flour

    3/2 tbps sugar

    3 tbsps melted butter

Filling

  • 16 ounce cream cheese

    1/2 cup white sugar

    zest of 2 lemons

    2 eggs

    1 tsp vanilla

    1/8 cup or 29 g lemon juice

    1/6 cup or 40 g heavy cream

    optional: lemon curd & whipped cream for topping

Directions

  1. do first: preheat oven to 350 F, melt butter, pulse lemon and sugar

    to mke crust, combine 3/4 cups graham cracker crumbs, 1/2 cups almond flour, 3/2 tbps sugar, 3 tbsps butter

    for the filling, beat 16 ounce cream cheese, 1/2 cups sugar, and zest of 2 lemons for around 2.5 min

    add in 2 whole eggs, one at a time

    once creamy, add 1 tsp vanilla, 1/8 cups or 29 g lemon juice, 1/6 cups or 40 g cups heavy cream and mix

    pour filling into still warm crust

    optional: use water bath to keep edges even

    bake for 55-65 min, with the center still jiggly but edges set

    turn off the oven and let rest for 1 hour, then transfer to cooling rack

    transfer to cooling rack and let it cool completely

    place in the fridge for 8 hrs (make sure to wrap it!)

    cut into squares and serve! optionally, top with lemon curd and whipped cream for extra yum !
